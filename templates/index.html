Sequence Alignment Studio

Compare DNA or protein sequences with global or local alignment, then inspect the diagnostic insights.

Why this setup matters

Different scoring choices can make the same pair of sequences look more or less biologically reasonable. This app lets you compare those choices quickly without exposing raw numeric parameters.

These presets are fetched live from NCBI for the spike gene coding region (DNA) and the spike protein sequences (protein). Choose DNA presets for DNA comparisons and protein presets for protein comparisons.
